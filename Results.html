Developed Programs

We made a tool for searching through the 2014 iGEM distribution: BioBrick Seeker.

We also made a iGEM Wiki Quickifier. that uploads a set of pages to the iGEM wiki and automatically adds all the parts common to all of them. This makes developing wiki pages easier as you can use better text editors and need to copy and paste a lot less.
